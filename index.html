How To Make Spaghetti

Ingredients:

  • Spaghetti Noodles
  • Maranara Sauce
  • Mozzarella Cheese
  • Parsley
  • Water
  • Salt

Instructions

  1. Take a pot and fill it with 4 quarts of water for every pound of noodles you have.
  2. Add a tablespoon of salt then heat up the water until it starts boiling.
  3. Add the noodles into the pot and stir regularly.
  4. Test if the noodle is cooked after the cooking time on the package by tasting it.
  5. Place the noodles inside a strainer and drain the water.
  6. Heat up the sauce in another pot and add the noodles once the sauce is warm.
  7. Mix thoroughly until all is well-heated
  8. Place on a plate, sprinkle parsley and mozzarella cheese.
  9. Ready to serve!
